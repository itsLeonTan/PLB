Our Story

Founded in 2024, PLB emerged from a simple observation: business needed to better tools to manage their financial operations. Traditional banking solutions were too rigid, while newer fintech offerings lacked the sophistication required for serious wealth management. Our team of financial experts and technology innovators came together to create a platform that combines the best of both worlds—cutting-edge technology with proven financial strategies.
